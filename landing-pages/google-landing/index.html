About Me

Email: rubengutierrezwebdesign@gmail.com

Hello! My name is Ruben Gutierrez & I'm a 29 year old Website Designer in the La Habra, California area, nice to meet you.

My story with technology started when I was 10 & I recieved my first computer for Christmas. When I wasn't taking it apart and putting it back together I was troubleshooting because It was always broken. Soon after I began to fix every computer I would come across making a decent amount of money.

Now that I'm older my skillset has greatly expanded. I've gone from resetting computers to being able to build websites and applications from scratch.

Working with computers in those early years of my life helped me gain an apperception for technology because I was able to improve the lives of those around me while being able to look out for myself.
